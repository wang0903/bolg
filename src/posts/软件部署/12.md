# Databasus MySQL备份

## 官网: https://databasus.com/

### 部署代码

```yaml

services:
  databasus:
    container_name: databasus
    image: swr.cn-north-4.myhuaweicloud.com/ddn-k8s/docker.io/databasus/databasus:v3.7.0
    ports:
      - "4005:4005"
    environment:
      - TZ=Asia/Shanghai
    volumes:
      - ./databasus-data:/databasus-data
    restart: unless-stopped
```
